# Datasets for ICAD

Thank you for your interest in the datasets associated with our TKDE paper: 
**"ICAD: Rethinking the Role of Inference and Cues for the Anomaly Detection of Time Series in IIoT"**.

This repository provides instructions on how to access the datasets used in our experiments.

---

## 1. The Proposed EO Dataset

The **EO dataset** provides time-series data from 430 sensors (e.g., temperature, flow rate, liquid level, pressure) that characterize a complex chemical production process. 

> **⚠️ IMPORTANT:** This is commercially sensitive data provided by our industrial partners. Its use is **strictly restricted to non-commercial academic research**.

### 📥 How to Access the EO Dataset
To better track the academic impact and ensure compliance with our data usage agreements, the EO dataset is **not publicly hosted** in this repository. 

If you would like to use the EO dataset for your academic research, please click the link below and fill out a brief request form. The download link will be provided to you upon completion.

## 2. Public Benchmark Datasets

Please note that while the SWaT, WADI, HAI, and PSM datasets are publicly accessible, direct redistribution by the authors is not permitted. Researchers must obtain these datasets through their official sources:

*   **SWaT & WADI:** Available at the [iTrust Labs Repository](https://itrust.sutd.edu.sg/itrust-labs_datasets/dataset_info/)
*   **HAI:** Available at the [Kaggle Repository](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/icsdataset/hai-security-dataset)
*   **PSM:** Collected by eBay and published in their proposed repository of anomaly detection models called RANSynCoders. Available at their [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/eBay/RANSynCoders/tree/main/data)
